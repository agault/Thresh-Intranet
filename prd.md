# Thresh IntraPortal
Feb 23, 2026

## Purpose
Thresh Portal is an internal web app that serves as a centralized digital workplace designed to eliminate information silos.

The primary objective is to **consolidate enterprise knowledge** and automate routine HR/Administrative tasks.

- Onboard new members
  - Reduce time to access team tools, data, and learning
- Assist product discovery
- Track operation optics

## Target Users
- **New Hires**
- Full-time Employees
- Contract Employees (Limited read/write)
- IT Admin: Manage permissions, security, and third-party integrations
- Leadership: Measure company health, employee/client satisfaction

## Features

### Onboarding
- Organization workchart
  - Who is everyone
  - What does each team do
  - Who to contact and How
- Personal + HR Actions
  - Pay stubs
  - PTO request
  - Benefits
  - IT Support

### Company Knowledge
- Market Trends and Technology News
- Communication-aids
  - Slack integration
  - Google Meets summaries
- Jira Integration: Ticketing search tool
- Federated search (indexing documents, wikis, and employee profiles across intraportal)
- File Management: Centralized repository with access permissions and advanced search for improved information management

### Security
- Data encryption (at rest and in transit)?
- Role-based access control
- Single Sign-On (SSO) Integration
- API Integration Layer: Connection to real-time payroll and employee records (Paylocity, Workday, etc.)

### Stretch-Goals after MVP
- Workflow Automation
- Insightful Dashboard: Optics, company performance, team capacity metrics for leadership
